Technical Project Manager
GreenTech Innovations
Odense, Denmark (Hybrid)

About GreenTech Innovations
GreenTech Innovations develops smart energy management systems for commercial buildings and industrial facilities. Our solutions help businesses reduce energy consumption by up to 30% while meeting sustainability goals. We are backed by leading Nordic venture capital firms and are rapidly scaling our operations.

The Opportunity
We are looking for a Technical Project Manager to lead the delivery of our software platform projects. You will bridge the gap between our engineering team and customers, ensuring projects are delivered on time, within scope, and to the highest quality standards.

What You'll Do
- Manage end-to-end delivery of software implementation projects
- Create and maintain project plans, schedules, and budgets
- Coordinate cross-functional teams including developers, QA, and DevOps
- Serve as the primary point of contact for customer stakeholders
- Identify risks and develop mitigation strategies
- Run agile ceremonies (sprint planning, retrospectives, daily standups)
- Track KPIs and report progress to senior management
- Continuously improve project management processes

What We're Looking For
- Bachelor's degree in Engineering, IT, or Business
- 5+ years of experience in technical project management
- PMP, PRINCE2, or Scrum Master certification
- Strong understanding of software development lifecycle
- Experience with Agile and Waterfall methodologies
- Proficiency in project management tools (Jira, Asana, MS Project)
- Excellent stakeholder management skills
- Strong written and verbal communication
- Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
- Danish and English fluency required

Bonus Points
- Experience in energy, IoT, or sustainability sectors
- Technical background (former developer or engineer)
- Experience with enterprise software implementations
- Knowledge of building management systems (BMS)

Compensation & Benefits
- Base salary: 55,000 - 70,000 DKK/month depending on experience
- Performance bonus up to 15% of annual salary
- Stock options in a high-growth company
- Flexible hybrid work (2-3 days in Odense office)
- 30 days annual leave
- Parental leave top-up
- Professional development allowance (20,000 DKK/year)
- Electric vehicle charging at office

To Apply
Submit your CV and a cover letter explaining your project management philosophy and a challenging project you successfully delivered.

We review applications on a rolling basis. Early applications encouraged.
